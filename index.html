The Islamic Center of Athens is currently working on several renovation projects to the residential areas, the bathrooms, the entrance and the outside sidewalks.

The residential rooms in the islamic center make up nearly all the income that the center receives to pay for all its expenses. During the last year, the islamic center struggled to get the residential rooms rented due to Covid-19 and due to the current state of the rooms. Currently, nearly %50 of the residential areas have been fully renovated and we are working on the remaining parts.

The severe decrease in income to the islamic center last year and the dire need for the renovation projects made us rely mostly on the generous donations from brothers and sisters like you.
